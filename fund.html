Application Process

Follow these 8 steps to get your project sponsored by Exposys Data Labs.

Step 1: Download Format

Download the official project synopsis format from our website.

Step 2: Prepare Abstract

Prepare your project abstract according to the required structure.

Step 3: Approval

Get the abstract approved and signed by your College Principal and HOD.

Step 4: Submission

Send the approved synopsis to hr@exposysdata.com.

Step 5: Document Review

Wait for the document verification process to be completed.

Step 6: Interview

Participate in the telephonic interview round with our technical team.

Step 7: Final Approval

Receive your project approval and welcome kit.

Step 8: Development

Begin the 45-day project development lifecycle with our mentors.

Evaluation Process

How we assess and select projects for sponsorship.

1. Submission

Initial review of your submitted synopsis and abstract.

2. Technical Review

Assessment of technical feasibility and innovation.

3. Interview

Telephonic discussion with the core development team.

4. Selection

Final confirmation and sponsorship allocation.
